Dive Into the Unknown

Unravel the horrific truth behind Ko'Verest Island

Across the islands luscious landscape lies an expansive network of abandoned buildings. You have been sent in to investigate the island as well as the disappearance of Delta squad. As the fierce combat operative Mike Ludwig, it is up to you to uncover the tragic events that took place and to conquer its evils.

Unleash Your Arsenal

Creatures Of War is a challenging 2D action-horror game. Monstrosities big and small are going to want a piece of you. Upgrade your guns, healing flask, and equip your grenades, its times to find Delta squad.

  • Intense action accompanied by the chilling threat of a brutal death lurking around every corner.
  • Gain access to the latest generation of grenades, tactically control the flow of battle, giving you the upper hand.
  • Collect treasures and money to fund weapon upgrades that will tear down even the biggest enemies.

Breathtaking Hand-Drawn Art

Creatures Of War is given life in strikingly grisly detail, the island is brought to life with prehistoric and terrifying creatures, all animated to masterful perfection.

Every biome that you'll visit is ruggedly unique and tough, bursting with new creatures and secrets to uncover. But Be Warned: Some secrets are better off left alone.

Diversify Your Weapon Collection

There are many different types of guns in Creatures Of War. Guns that will fit into nearly any playstyle. If you prefer to kill from a distance, equip a bolt action rifle, feel the need to get up close and personal? The shotgun has you covered. Each weapon type will have distinct characteristics from its peers of the same weapon subclass, each gun is unique, no choice is wrong.

Creatures Of War's Core Values

These core values are the lifeblood of this game's development.

Combat

Painstakingly balanced combat focusing on ultra responsive controls, shockingly intelligent ai and tactical decision making. We aim to create stellar 2D combat through iterative and community based feedback.

Aggression

Opportunities exist to create dynamic and engaging worlds when such evil lurks around every corner. Will you cower in fear, or stand on your own two legs?

Alive

When bringing a world to life you need music and sound effects that resonate with the environments you are exploring. We have hand crafted every SFX and musical note you hear to deliver results that will bring every territory you explore to life.

Mike Ludwig

Charlie teams tactical expert, he is trained to deliver results in the most extreme circumstances. Proficient with all types of firearms, Mike is very resourceful and if need be is not afraid to run away from a losing conflict. He was assigned this mission for a reason, he can get the job done.

Bio Organic Weapons

Satellite Images have revealed these creatures to us, we need to find out what else resides on Ko'Vorest Island.

The Amargonyx is the result of genetic tampering between an Allosaurus and a Titanosaur. The alarming size of this creature makes their hides extremely thick, making them all the harder to kill. They are very ill tempered creatures and will hunt their prey to the death after being provoked. Their long necks allow for crushing blows to take place from unsuspecting distances.

The biggest and baddest dinosaur on the island, at least at first. The T-Rex is the epitome of power and death. One swift bite is capable of ripping its victims to shreds. The typical reaction is to run, but recent testing has shown that the T-Rex can vastly outrun any human. It is highly advisable to proceed with caution if a T-Rex is thought to be in the area.

The Bulletwolf Ant Queen has only been known to inhabit the deepest and warmest chambers of their bulletwolf ant farms. They lay eggs at an alarming rate and have voracious appetites that never slow down. The worst part about them is they don't do the hunting and their combat capabilities are largely unknown.

The Utah Raptor is the velociraptor's big older brother, as they are taller, heftier, and wield longer claws. These factors alone make this an intimidating foe all by themselves, but of course the Utah Raptor happens to be a pack hunter.

Death from above and below. The Giant Asian Hornoraptor delivers a sting equally painful as it is poisonous. The victims are consumed on site. Long claws, viscious teeth, and that harrowing sound when one is flying in the air will send chills down your spine.

Quick and small, pack hunter, slightly venomous, and surprisingly aggressive. The procompsognathus does not have the hides or the offensive capabilities of other creatures on Ko'Vorest island, but what it lacks in lethality is made up in numbers. If you encounter one of these creatures, expect the entire family.

The Gigantus Wandering Spitting Spider has been genetically engineered from two genus of arachnids to create the ultimate spider. Crippling poison, engulfing webs shot from distance, and a fang size that can serrate any flesh it encounters briefly describes this creature's terrifying capabilities.
